About

A simulator developed based on a discrete modeling framework for simulating gradient-driven advection -dispersion-reaction dynamics of multi species transport occuring in hierarchical networks found in biology.

The project is developed by the Biomolecular Computation LaboratoryIISC Deepa Mahesvare M. DEEPA, Prof. Debnath Pal D.PAL and by the Scientific Computation Laboratory including Prof. Soumyendu Raha at Department of Computational and Data Sciences IISC, Indian Institute of Science IISc-Bangalore. The team of contributors include Subhraneel Haldar, Pranav, Rohit Chakraborty and Rohit Pandey at .. (inlcude cds logo , iisc IISc-Bangalorelogo)

The study is supported by intramural funds provided by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. Research of DM is supported by the Senior Research Fellowship from the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India.

Please use the following article to cite this project: Deepa Maheshvare, M., Raha, S. and Pal, D., 2022. A graph-based framework for multiscale modeling of physiological transport. Frontiers in Network Physiology, p.18. DOI: https://doi.org/10.3389/fnetp.2021.802881
